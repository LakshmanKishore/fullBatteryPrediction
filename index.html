Full Battery Time Prediction

Details

This web App predicts the time for full charge of the device.
Based on the data collected while the device is in charging mode.

How to Use?

● Keep the device to charge.
● Press the Collect Data button.
● Wait for 2 mins.
● Now the data(Charging Level at Certain Interval) is collected.
● Press the Predict Time button.
● The Time for the device to finish 100% charge is approximately predicted and displayed on the screen.
